About

I am a second-year PhD student at the Queensland University of Technology, in the School of Mathematics. My research focuses on Player List Management Optimisation in the Australian Football League. This combines operations research and statistics principles to discover optimal strategies around list management. At this stage, I have looked at how goal kicking accuracy differs between players using an ordinal regression model and I have developed a win probability model. I am now focusing on creating a basic optimisation model using an initial team construction as an input and looking at which players may be added to the team to improve the chance of winning.

Outside my PhD, I enjoy watching the AFL and a number of other sports.

I have graduated with a Bachelor of Mathematics (Operations Research) (Honours) and a Bachelor of Business (Finance).
